READING COMPREHENSION

Moths are not liked by many people because they look ugly. When people light their rooms up at night, moths become a great nuisance as they happily swarm around the roof. If one catches them, they leave a powdery substance on the person's palms. This substance comes from their wings.

If a family uses a candle, a lamp or any other homemade lighting system, moths often put them out, leaving the family in darkness. To some people, moths are supposed to be killed at once, even to the extent of using insecticide. Early in the morning, moths are so weakened by the cold that they cannot move.

People who keep chickens invite them in to feed on the moths. But how many people know that moths are useful to them?

When moths lay their eggs on tree branches and twigs, they hatch into caterpillars. Caterpillars are protein-giving food and are widely eaten in many parts of Zambia. Caterpillars are of different types. People should learn to like and preserve moths as they are a source of food.
