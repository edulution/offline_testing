READING COMPREHENSION

HOW TO GET RID OF RATS

When I was a young man, working in Senanga, my boss gave me a difficult job to do. The roof of his house had become infected with rats and he wanted me to get rid of them. I tried everything: rat poison, cats, even a mongoose, but I failed to remove them.

Then a friend of mine advised me to use a python. I considered this to be my last chance and agreed to try it. My friend brought me a box in which he had trapped a young python about six feet long. We managed to get the box into the roof and then released the python. The effect was amazing! The rats disappeared. It proved to be a wonderful solution. But we didn't know what to do with the python. I couldn't shoot and kill the creature that had helped me. It took the six of us an hour before we managed to get it into the box and then we returned it to the jungle.
