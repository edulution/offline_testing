This land will be filled with SWAZIS from the North; and Swaziland will be occupied by Xosas, Tembus and Finguoes from the South. The next couple of years will be devoted to this work. The empire will unite even as it expands. I also command that my people should go to school to learn the white man's knowledge. In ten years to come, I will conquer ignorance with the same zeal as I have conquered all our enemies. I have built a nation Dingiswayo, I will never weaken. There will be killing of the cowards and the weak until knowledge can keep us strong on its own.
