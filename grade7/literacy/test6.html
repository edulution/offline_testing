READING COMPREHENSION

One day when Sipho was at school, he saw one of his friends smoking something. It looked like tobacco. So, Sipho asked his friend to give him a cigarette so that he could feel the way people who smoke tobacco feel. His friend gave him a cigarette. He took a puff and started coughing. After a few minutes, Sipho started running around the school shouting and chasing other learners. Then, he started to feel dizzy and sick. The teacher came in and asked him what was wrong. He could not tell him because he was feeling dizzy and was panting. The teacher took Sipho to the clinic. At the clinic the nurse examined him and told the teacher that he had smoked dagga. The following day, he felt better. He told himself never to smoke tobacco or dagga because it made him feel sick.
