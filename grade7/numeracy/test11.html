ASSESSMENT
WORD PROBLEMS

(1) A truck collected 2 450 bags of maize at one maize shed and 250 bags of maize at another maize shed. How many bags of maize were collected altogether?

(2) In a certain year, Mrs Chongo produced 10 000 bags of rice while Mrs Mubita produced 9 874 bags of rice. Calculate the difference between the number of bags of rice produced.

(3) If 7 packets of salt cost K49.00, what will be the cost of 13 packets of the same type?

(4) 8 boys can paint a hall in 6 hours. How many hours can 12 boys take to paint the same hall, if they work at the same rate?

(5) 105 people used a bus for a trip. If the bus carried 35 people only at a time, how many trips did it make?

(6) Mutale had K3 000.00 to spend. She bought two dresses at K500.00, two belts at K90.00, a handbag at K250.00 and a pair of shoes at K499.00. How much money did she remain with?

(7) Given that ＄1 is equal to K6.00, what is the price in Zambian Kwacha of a car costing ＄2 125?

(8) Find the sum of 718 624 and 137 392.

(9) 340 textbooks were shared equally among 10 schools. How many textbooks did each school receive?

(10) 6 boxes of wool cost K180.00. How much will 4 boxes of the same type of wool cost?

(11) Mary took 10 small stones every day and left them at school. How many stones did she take to school in 30 days?

(12) A group of boys had 23 guavas altogether. They ate Y guavas and remained with 18. How many guavas did they eat?

(13) A man bought a packet of sugar at K11.00, a loaf of bread at K4.50 and a packet of milk at K3.75. How much did he spend altogether?

(14) 8 men can do a piece of work in 12 days. How long will it take 12 men to do the same work?

(15) There are two boxes of oranges. One of the boxes contains 144 oranges while the other contains 156 oranges. How many oranges are packed in the boxes altogether?

(16) A school has 1 575 pupils. If there are 45 pupils in every class, how many classes are there in the school?

(17) Joyce and Jane bought 20 100 pockets of cement altogether. If Jane got 9 001 pockets of cement, how many pockets of cement did Joyce get?

(18) A wholesaler delivered 204 crates of bottles of soft drinks to a shop. If one crate had 24 bottles of soft drinks, how many bottles of soft drinks were delivered?

(19) Mwale has K80.00 and buys 4 loaves of bread at K4.00 each, 2 packets of sugar at K12.00 each and fish for K35.00. What is Mwale's change?

(20) Tithandizane Women's group received 2 247 bags of rice. If the club used 176 bags, how many bags of rice remained?

(21) Two co-operatives in a province produced 28 321 and 62 477 bags of maize. What was the total number of bags produced altogether?

(22) Mr Kalenga harvested 1 500 bags of maize from his farm. Mr Phiri harvested three times as much as Mr Kalenga. How many bags did Mr Phiri harvest?

(23) A room has 15 shelves each, the shelf can hold 345 books of the same type. How many books of the same type can be put in this room altogether?

(24) A lady bought 2 dresses at K35 000 each and 3 books at K5 000 each. How much change did she get after paying K100 000?

(25) A brick has a mass of 3 149g What is the total mass of 12 such bricks?

(26) The product of two numbers is 51/4. One of the numbers is 1/4. Find the other number

(27) A man has a water bill of K64 000 and an electricity bill of K105 000. What is his total bill?

(28) In an election. The number of votes for three members of parliament were 5 464, 18 601 and 12 371. How many votes did they get altogether?

(29) If a passenger train carries 2 143 passengers in a day, how many passengers can it carry in 15 days if it carries the same number of passengers?

(30) A school bought 274 books. 186 of these books were given to the pupils. How many books were not given out?
