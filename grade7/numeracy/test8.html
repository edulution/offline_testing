AREA

VOLUME

(24) A cuboid is of length 9cm, width 7cm and height 5cm. Calculate its volume.

(25) The shape of a water tank at Mrs Bupe's farm is that of a cuboid. If its length is 14m, breadth 12m and height 10m, find the volume of the water tank.

(26) A rectangular water tank measures 7cm long and 5cm wide. If its height is 4cm, what is its volume?

(27) Find the volume of a rectangular prism measuring 6cm long, 4cm wide and 2cm high.

(28) A cuboid has a volume of 240cm3. If the length of the cuboid is 10cm and the width is 6cm, find the height.

(29) A container measures 4m long, 2m wide and 2m high. How many small boxes each measuring 2m long, 2m wide and 1m high can be packed in the container?

(30) A Bible is 16 cm wide, 20cm long and 7cm thick. What is the volume of this Bible?
